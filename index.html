About Uganda

Uganda is ‘gifted by nature’. It is a home to over 14 primate species, nearly 150 mammals and over 1,000 bird species. It has spectacular scenery and this makes Uganda a paradise to those who loves nature. Uganda is multicultural country in East Africa whose diverse landscape encompasses the snowcapped Rwenzori Mountains and immense Lake Victoria - the principal source of the great River Nile. With abundant wildlife such as Chimpanzees, Gorillas, famous Big 5 at our 10 Savannah parks, and 1000+ rare bird species. Bwindi Impenetrable Forest National Park is a renowned mountain gorilla sanctuary, while Kidepo Valley National contends for best destination in Africa for wild game drives. Apart from just amazing nature,Uganda has more to offer including and it includes the following.

  • 01 Outdoor activities

    Uganda has various outdoor activities like hiking, kayaking, white water rafting, mountain biking among other

  • 02Diverse Culture

    With over 50 tribes all with their own customs and traditions. Visit schools, farms and cultural/ religious sites or try your hand at cookery, drumming or craft making. All with the famously friendly and welcoming Ugandan people.

  • 02Rest and relaxtion

    After all that, you might be in need of a little rest and relaxation and Uganda has that covered too. Why not spend a couple of days at the beach, in the Sesse Islands, relaxing in the sunshine and indulging in a little pampering in an exclusive spa?

  • 03Thriving Modern Culture

    Which a tour of Kampala will quickly reveal as you visit some of the trendiest shops, galleries, cafes, bars and restaurants and dance the night away in some of the hottest clubs.
